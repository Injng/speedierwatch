How the Referral System Works

Cascading Referral Rewards

Our referral system rewards you for not just your direct referrals, but for anyone they refer as well! The benefits cascade up to 5 levels deep.

How Tickets Are Calculated
Base Tickets

You earn 2 tickets for each correct answer on the quiz.

Example: 7 correct answers = 14 base tickets

Calculation: 7 Ã— 2 = 14

Referral Bonuses

You receive 20% of the tickets earned by people you refer, and this continues up to 5 levels deep!

Level Who Bonus You Receive
Level 1 Direct referral (your friend) 20% of their tickets
Level 2 Your friend's referral 20% of the 20% bonus (4% of original)
Level 3 Your friend's friend's referral 20% of the Level 2 bonus (0.8% of original)
Level 4 3 steps removed 20% of the Level 3 bonus (0.16% of original)
Level 5 4 steps removed 20% of the Level 4 bonus (0.032% of original)
Full Example

If you refer Alice who scores 8 on the quiz (earning 16 tickets):

  • You get 20% of Alice's tickets: 3.2 tickets
  • If Alice refers Bob who scores 10 (earning 20 tickets):
    • Alice gets 4 tickets (20% of 20)
    • You get 0.8 tickets (20% of Alice's 4 tickets)
  • This continues through more referrals, with each level earning you a smaller percentage
Pro Tip: To maximize your tickets, refer people who you think will score highly on the quiz and who will also refer others! This creates a network effect that benefits everyone.
